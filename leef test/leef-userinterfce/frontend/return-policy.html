1. 📋 Eligibility for Refunds

You may be eligible for a refund if your order meets the following conditions:

  • The item delivered was damaged, spoiled, or significantly different from what was ordered.
  • The complaint is raised within 48 hours of the delivery timestamp.
  • You provide photographic evidence of the issue at the time of reporting.
  • The order was placed through the leef platform and payment was confirmed.

🟢 Tip: Always inspect your order upon delivery and report issues immediately to speed up processing.

2. 🚫 Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be issued in the following cases:

  • Complaints raised after 48 hours of delivery.
  • Items that were accepted at delivery without noting any issues to the driver.
  • Change of mind or incorrect quantity ordered by the customer.
  • Items that were stored improperly after delivery.
  • Orders placed using promotional or discounted pricing unless the product is genuinely faulty.

⚠️ COD orders: Refunds for COD payments will be issued as leef wallet credit only.

3. 🔄 How to Request a Refund
1

Submit a Refund Request

Go to your Dashboard → Refunds → New Request. Select the affected order and describe the issue.

2

Attach Evidence

Upload clear photographs of the damaged or incorrect items. This is mandatory for approval.

3

Seller Review

The seller is notified and given 24 hours to accept or dispute the refund request.

4

Admin Decision

leef admin reviews and makes a final decision. Approved refunds are processed within 3–5 business days.

4. 💳 Refund Methods

Approved refunds are returned via one of the following:

  • Card Payments: Refunded to the original card within 5–7 business days.
  • COD Payments: Refunded as leef loyalty coins / store credit.
  • Partial Refunds: If only part of an order is affected, only the affected portion is refunded.
5. 📞 Contact for Disputes

If your refund request is denied and you believe the decision was incorrect, contact our support team within 7 days of the denial notice. We will escalate to a senior reviewer.
