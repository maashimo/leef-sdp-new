1. 📂 Information We Collect

When you use leef, we collect the following types of information:

Category Examples Purpose
Account Data Name, email, phone, password (hashed) Authentication, account management
Order Data Items ordered, delivery address, order history Fulfillment, invoice generation
Payment Data Payment method type, transaction reference Payment recording (no raw card data stored)
Usage Data Pages visited, time on site, device type Platform improvement, analytics
Feedback Data Reviews, ratings, uploaded photos Product quality monitoring

🔵 We never store raw credit card numbers. Payments are processed securely and only transaction references are retained.

2. ⚙️ How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders and payments.
  • Send order confirmation, delivery, and refund notifications.
  • Manage your loyalty coin balance and rewards.
  • Provide customer support and resolve disputes.
  • Improve our platform through aggregated analytics.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

We do not use your data for automated decision-making or profiling for third-party advertising.

3. 🤝 Data Sharing

We share your data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Sellers: Your name, delivery address, and order details are shared with the relevant seller to fulfil your order.
  • Delivery Partners: Basic contact and location information shared for delivery coordination.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose data if required by Sri Lankan law or a valid court order.

⚠️ We never sell your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. 🔐 Data Security

We implement the following measures to protect your data:

  • Passwords are stored using industry-standard hashing (bcrypt).
  • All data transmission is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS.
  • Database access is restricted to authorised backend services only.
  • Regular security reviews and dependency updates.

Despite these measures, no system is 100% secure. If you suspect a breach of your account, contact us immediately.

5. 🧑‍⚖️ Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the following rights:

👁️

Access

Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.

✏️

Correction

Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.

🗑️

Deletion

Request deletion of your account and data (subject to legal holds).

📤

Portability

Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

🛑

Objection

Object to specific processing activities.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@leef.lk.

6. 🍪 Cookies & Local Storage

leef uses browser localStorage (not third-party tracking cookies) to store:

  • Your login session (user object)
  • Cart contents
  • Checkout preferences

This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to advertising networks.

7. 🗓️ Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion:

  • Personal profile data is deleted within 30 days.
  • Order and payment records are retained for 7 years for legal / tax compliance.
  • Feedback and reviews are anonymised and may remain visible.
